PREVENTION TECHNIQUES

  • Primary prevention aims to prevent disease or injury before it ever occurs
  • Secondary prevention aims to reduce impact of a disease or injury that has already occurred

Disease: Flu

  • Primary Prevention:
  • Get immunized
  • Wash hands to prevent spread
  • Cover mouth and nose when sneezing or coughing
  • Keep surroundings clean

    • Secondary Prevention
  • Antiviral drugs to cure
  • Follow doctor’s instruction
  • HIV/AIDS

    • Primary Prevention:
  • Don’t use unclean needles or unscreened blood
  • Protected sex with usage of male or female condom

    • Secondary Prevention:
  • Anti-retroviral regimens
